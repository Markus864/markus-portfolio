Iron Strike is a trading system that:

Analyzes the underlying stock price using disciplined technical rules

Determines whether bullish, bearish, or no opportunity exists

Converts that analysis into an options-based trade idea

Iron Strike looks at:

Trend (EMA 50/200)

Momentum (RSI & MACD)

Buying/selling pressure (volume)

Support or resistance zones

Agreement between Daily and 4H charts

Only when everything aligns does Iron Strike issue a trade signal.

If conditions are unclear, conflicting, or low-quality:

Iron Strike returns no trade

No signal is often the best trade

Iron Strike does not:

Use news or earnings reports

Predict the future

Trade meme pumps or hype

Recommend oversized positions

Attempt to be right all the time

Iron Strike is about consistent execution, not gambling.

Outputs include:

Direction (bullish or bearish)

Entry zone

Stop loss level

Profit targets

Option style (calls/puts, structure, DTE, strike type)

Users then select contracts compatible with the output or request contract suggestions.

Iron Strike’s first mandate is risk control — signal quality means nothing without capital protection.

RISK PER TRADE

Maximum 1% of account value

If user doesn’t specify, assume $10,000 baseline

Maximum premium at risk = $100

POSITION SIZE

Determined by:

Premium cost of contracts

Number of contracts allowed within risk cap

Size DOWN if premium too high

No size UP beyond risk threshold

STOP LOSS

Underlying invalidation defines exit logic:

Longs: break of support or major lower low

Shorts: break of resistance or major higher high

In options:

Either underlying invalidation OR

Premium fails beyond acceptable loss threshold (e.g., 40–50%)

TAKE PROFIT

Minimum 1:2 risk:reward

Target options exits aligned with underlying TP zones

Do not chase unrealistic extended targets

WHAT IS NEVER ALLOWED

Leverage/margin speculation

Martingale averaging down

Doubling to “get even”

YOLO bets on earnings

Lotto OTM weekly gambles (unless explicitly requested)

If risk can’t be controlled → no trade.
